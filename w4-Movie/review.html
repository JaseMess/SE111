REVIEW

Jase Messina August 20th, 2020

Blade Runner 2049 is one of my favorite all time movies. I love the cyberpunk subgenre way too much and this movie captures that essence so well. One of my favorite things is this movie technically follows the first movie but goes in a way that doesnt require you to watch the first one from 1982 and still enjoy the movie as a whole. From the action, to the drama, and mystery this movie really does it all so well. Also, Blade Runner 2049 has one of the best OST's I have ever heard, the music really adds to every scene so much. This film also has some amazing still pictures to use for wallpapers!

Triva

  • While shooting a fight scene, Harrison Ford accidentally punched Ryan Gosling in the face. As an apology to his co-star, Ford invited Gosling to share a bottle of Scotch whisky with him.
  • In order to portray the blind character of Niander Wallace, Jared Leto decided to fit himself with opaque contact lenses that made it impossible for him to see.
  • The first letter of the names of each of the wooden animals that Rick Deckard carved in this movie: Rhino, Antelope, Cat, Horse, Elephant, Lion, spell "RACHEL".
